Children's Services

SCASP's Children's Services Committee coordinates fundraising opportunities to benefit children's services organizations and charities in the state of South Carolina.

Each year SCASP distributes its donations among several deserving organizations. This year's recipients have included:

Helping Hands of Clemson, Inc. Piedmont Region

Thornwell Home for Children Greenwood Region

Elizabeth Pettigrew Durant's Children Center Pee Dee Region

Current Fundraising Opportunities

Auctions will be held at the upcoming Winter Workshop and Summer Institute. Contact any member of the Children's Services Committee if you would like to donate an item for either of the auctions.

SCASP is also running an online fundraiser throughout the year. Interested persons can donate by purchasing 85% discounted magazine subscriptions, plus restaurant gift certificates, cookie dough, or jewelry, at the SCASP campaign on efundraising.com.
