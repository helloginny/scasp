SCASP Event: Spring Workshop - March 23, 2012

SCASP is approved by the National Association of School Psychologists to offer continuing education for school psychologists. SCASP maintains responsibility for the program.

Workshop Details

Please join us for the 2012 SCASP Spring Workshop "Executive Functions and Classroom Behavior" presented be George McCloskey, PhD, of the Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine. The workshop will take place at the DiGiorgio Campus Center at Winthrop University in Rock Hill, SC.

This workshop will present a comprehensive model of executive functions and discuss the impact of executive functions on learning, behavior and classroom production. Ways to assess executive function difficulties and methods for helping children with EF difficulties improve their behavior and academic performance either through teacher assistance or through increasing their capacity for self regulation also will be addressed.
