SCASP Membership Benefits

  1. Receive School Psych Scene (SCASP newsletter) delivered to your mailbox 6 times a year.
  2. Reduced conference fees (Fall and Spring).
  3. Stay informed of the issues that impact South Carolina School Psychologists.
  4. Recertification credit or Professional growth opportunities.
  5. Celebrate the successes accomplished by school psychologists.
  6. Opportunity to network with people who really understand you.
  7. Learn about the most recent professional practices, publications, and technology.
  8. Access employment opportunities and career development information.
  9. Impact local activities through regional groups.

Applying for SCASP Membership

The SCASP membership year runs from January 1 to December 31. New dues submitted July 1-November 30 are 1/2 of those stated; renewals are full price. Dues submitted December 1-31 apply to the following year. Please review the SCASP bylaws and constitution for additional information concerning SCASP membership.

Send application, payment, and required documentation to:

SCASP Membership Chairperson | P.O. Box 1177 | Irmo, SC 29063

New Membership

To apply for new membership with SCASP, either complete the online application or mail a copy of the new membership application to the Membership Chairperson at the address above.

If you are applying for Student Membership, you must also include one letter of recommendation from a faculty member confirming student status with your application. Those applying for Full Membership must include a copy of their state certification, and those applying for Associate Membership status must include one letter of recommendation from a certified school psychologist.

Upon receipt, the membership chairperson will review your application to ensure you have met criteria for the status for which you are applying. The SCASP board will confirm your application.


(Optional) Pay online through PayPal. Please include a copy of your payment receipt if you are mailing other components of your application. Please note: A PayPal processing fee will be included with online payments.

Existing Membership Renewal

To renew your membership with SCASP, either complete the online renewal application or mail a copy of the new membership application to the Membership Chairperson at the address above.

Students who have become employed full-time in the last year are eligible for Full membership. Please enclose a copy of your certificate with your application for membership.


(Optional) Pay online through PayPal. Please include a copy of your payment receipt if you are mailing other components of your application. Please note: A PayPal processing fee will be included with online payments.
